Domain Brokerage Business Workflow
You never buy domains. You find expired domains → find buyers → broker the deal → earn commission.
Step 1: Discover (automated via Discovery page)
- Enter niche (ai, saas, finance, etc.)
- Click "Run Discovery"
- System scrapes ExpiredDomains.net + GoDaddy auctions
- Analyzes DR, backlinks, age, SEO, commercial value
- Scores each domain 0-100 (Broker Score)
- Grades: Hot Lead / Warm / Lukewarm / Cold
Step 2: Analyze (automated in pipeline)
- For each domain, system runs:
- SEO Analysis — DR, referring domains, age
- History Check — adult/gambling/malware history via Wayback Machine
- Commercial Analysis — category, brandability, keyword value
- Broker Analysis — estimated value, commission calculation, buyer lead research
Step 3: Find Buyers (via Discovery page results)
- System searches LinkedIn, Google, Crunchbase for companies in that niche
- Generates buyer lead list with contact info
- Example: For vetclinicfinder.com → finds veterinary clinic companies
Step 4: Outreach (via Outreach page)
- System generates personalized email templates
- Buyer template: "I found a domain perfect for your business..."
- You send emails manually (or configure SMTP)
- Track status: pending → sent → replied → negotiating → closed_won
Step 5: Close Deal (via Deals page)
- When buyer agrees, generate commission agreement
- Set commission rate (typically 10-15%)
- Buyer purchases domain from registrar
- You collect commission
Step 6: Track Everything
- Dashboard shows pipeline stats
- Grade distribution, top opportunities
- Deal status, commission tracking
- Purchase history with ROI
To start right now:
1. Open http://localhost:8501
2. Go to Discovery → pick a niche → click "Run Discovery"
3. Review scored domains → click on a Hot Lead
4. Go to Outreach → send emails to buyers
5. Go to Deals → generate agreement when they agree
